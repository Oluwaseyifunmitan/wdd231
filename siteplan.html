Site Plan - Pathway Student Guide

Pathway Student Guide

Pathway Student Guide is a dedicated resource hub offering study tips, helpful materials, and shared experiences to support students in the Pathway program.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide easily accessible guidance, encouragement, and tools for students currently navigating their Pathway journey. From study help to motivational stories, the site will serve as a support system for students striving for success.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What study strategies have helped former Pathway students succeed?

Scenario 2: Where can I find encouraging stories or testimonials from other Pathway students?
